Aesthetic satisfaction

Simple to complex

One day, I saw a research data showing that a person's aesthetic satisfaction is related to the fractal index. The theory is that humans feel the most beauty when the fractal index is 1.4. In Asia, on the contrary, there is saying "the beauty of blank space" which is a concept that has been passed down for a long time that the more it is emptied and reduced, the more beautiful it is. I was able to empathize with both of these two conflicting concepts, and I organized the cased that fit those ideas into a graph.

N.B. Only works drawn on a flat surface are represented.
