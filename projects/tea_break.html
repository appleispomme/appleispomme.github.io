Tea break

Take a deep breath and drink your tea.

A project that started with the question of how to relieve the burden of modern society with one small action. At the time, in Korea and Japan, single person households were growing, and the number of people who cooked with healthy ingredients was extremely small. Since convenience stores opened until late at night, many people resorted to instant food for their meals. This project contains a message to provide a light, if not fancy, tea break to people who live with high expectations in society and within their families, eating foods that are burdensome to their stomach (high calories).
